Chord Synonyms

Due to the fact that the guitar player is res tricted to play chords with a maximum of 6 notes at a time and a minimum of 3 (at least for full chords) sometimes compromises have to be done and notes have to be left out. This leaves space for chord synonyms, mostly invertions of each other:
(This list is by no means complete) 
  
  C / E                  Cmaj
  C / D                  D9(sus4/11)
  C / F                  Fmaj9
  C / A                  Amin7
  Cadd9 (no 3rd)         Gsus4
  Cadd9 sus 4            G7sus4
  Cmin / A#              D#6
  C6                     Amin / C
  C6                     Am7
  C6/9 (no root)         A7sus4
  Cmin6                  Amin7b5 / C       
  C7                     C7/6
  C7b9 (no root)         Edim
  Cmaj7                  Emin / C
  C9b5 (no root)         Gb 7+5
  C9b5                   D9+5
  Cmaj9                  G / C
  Cm9  (no root)         Eb maj7
