NOTES:
Well I hope this isn't confusing.  Once you get comfortable with the intro,
the rest is pretty easy.  I left out the 2nd solo.  Sorry!  Try improvising  
that part. The 1st solo is pretty close to accurate I think.  I faked some 
parts, but it's close enough.  At the end of that solo, go to the 19th fret
and slide down while picking swiftly.  This takes some practice, but it's 
cool when you get it down.  Also, add the chorus riffs freely during the 
G to F# to F.  Other than that it's relatively easy.  Good luck.  If this 
gets posted on any site, please give me credit.  Any questions, comments, or
critiques, feel free to e-mail me:
